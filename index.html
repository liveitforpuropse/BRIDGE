Who We Are

Our Story

Bridge started as a simple idea among friends — that a group of individuals, each contributing whatever they can, could together change the course of a student's life.

We are not an NGO. We have no PAN card, no formal registration. We are four working professionals — Rajani, Indu, Shashi, and Niveda — who believe in personal social responsibility. We put in our own time, our own effort, outside of our regular jobs, because we believe every student deserves a fair shot at education.

Over three years, we have built a community of 100+ like-minded Bridge members who respond to every student request with generosity — directly paying college and school fees, no cash, no middlemen, ever.

PSR at Heart
Personal Social Responsibility — from the heart

Zero Cash Policy

Fees are paid directly to the college or school. No cash is given to students, parents, or anyone in between. Ever.

Verified Requests Only

Every application is reviewed by the core team. Students must submit fee receipts, student ID, mark sheets, Aadhaar, and income certificate.

No Minimum Contribution

Anyone in the Bridge group can contribute any amount — ₹100 or ₹10,000. Every rupee goes directly toward the student's fees.

Volunteer-Run, Always

The core team works outside their regular jobs — no salaries, no overhead, no admin fees. 100% of contributions reach students.

The Process

How Bridge Works

From the first WhatsApp message to the fee being paid — here's the full journey.

1

Student Reaches Out via WhatsApp

A student (or their parent/guardian) contacts the Bridge Core Group via WhatsApp, briefly explaining their situation and need for financial assistance.

2

Application Form Sent

The core team shares a standardized application form. The student fills in:

Name, Age, Phone
Course / Institution
Fee Amount & Reason
Income Certificate
Bona fide / Fee Receipt
Student ID + Mark Sheet
Aadhaar (Student + Parent)
Description / Story
3

Core Group Reviews & Approves

Rajani, Indu, Shashi, and Niveda review all submitted documents, verify the details, and make an approval decision. Genuine, verified cases are approved.

4

Request Posted in Bridge Main Group

Once approved, the student's request is shared in the Bridge Main Group (100+ members). The post includes the student's story, fee requirement, and UPI details of the institution for direct transfer.

5

Members Contribute Freely

Bridge members contribute whatever they can via PhonePe or Google Pay. There is no minimum or maximum. The community rallies together until the fee is covered.

6

Fee Paid Directly to Institution

The fee is paid directly to the college or school. No cash, no interim transfers to parents or students. This is a strict, non-negotiable rule of Bridge.

ZERO CASH POLICY — Fees go only to the institution, always.
7

Contribution Summary Shared

After payment, the student shares the contribution summary — who contributed, how much, and the fee payment confirmation — back with the Bridge group. Full transparency, always.
